The College of West Anglia’s hair and beauty students train in specialist Affinage salons at the King’s Lynn and Wisbech campuses. These outstanding facilities offer a range of low priced treatments available to the public, as well as staff and students.
We offer a full range of award-winning services for hair, nails and beauty for men and women. Hairdressing students provide a wide range of shampoo, cuts, styles and colours. Beauty students offer a full-range of beauty treatments,including facial and body treatments, hair removal, massage and tanning treatments.
